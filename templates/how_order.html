How to Order

  • Go to Create Order
  • Select the minifigures you want to buy
  • After selecting, click View Invoice
  • Fill in the necessary details: Name, Phone Number, Address, Delivery Area

    • ***Must provide ONE of the Social Contacts***

  • After filling up, click Confirm Order
  • Click OK to lock in your order
  • Tap OK and relax! Your Minifigures are on their way
