How to Order from the BLOCKS Website

  • Go to Create Order


  • Select the minifigures you want to buy


  • After selecting, click View Invoice and fill in the necessary details (Name, Phone Number, Address)


  • Then click Print Invoice or take a screenshot of the invoice and send it to our Facebook or WhatsApp chat to confirm the order. Our team will confirm it.
